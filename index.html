We are among the women who contribute to open source.

We started meeting with Katrina Owens in April 2015. She was nice enough to work with us on her excellent open source project exercism.io which is a site where you can practice your coding skills in multiple languages and get feedback about your code.
As the site states:

"Exercism began as a way to support students learning to code by giving them practice problems and real world feedback."
